About Us

Above-Average Friendships was started in 1997 by Todd Sopaczewicz & Randy LeSean to make using and working with computers easier and better. The Internet (and now the World Wide Web) have revolutionized how people interact with each other. Our company is dedicated to giving people access to computers and the Internet to help with their business, tell people there interests, and connect the world together.

Todd Sopaczewicz Co-Founder, Manager

Todd is a Pennsylvania native who has always had a keen interest in electronics and technology. He decided to use that passion to help people see the potential in the Internet the way he does, and to bring knowledge and access to people. He has over ten years experience working with customers on computer problems, as well as being an early user of the Information Super-highway. In his spare time, he likes to build PCs, tinker with transistor radios, and has a budding interest in soapmaking at home. Todd attended Kutztown University, where he earned a B.S. in Business Administration.

Randy LeSean Co-Founder, Lead Programmer

Randy is from Union, New Jersey, where he spent his early days working as a clerk at his father's shop. There he learned the ins and outs of running a business. He moved on to work as a computer programmer for an outfit that works with banks, where he learned a great deal about the finance world. He spends his spare time relaxing at home and spending time with his three children and wife. Randy has an A.A.B in Finance from Rutgers University, Camden.
